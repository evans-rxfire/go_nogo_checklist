Prescribed Fire Go / No-Go Checklist

If the answer to any item below is "NO", DO NOT burn until corrected.

Go / No-Go Checklist

1. Is the Burn Boss on site for the proposed burn?

2. Is a PBB-approved written burn plan established for the proposed burn?

3. Are all safety requirements met for the proposed burn?

4. Are all planned personnel and equipment on site?

5. Have all entities in the county where the burn is to take place been notified?

a. County Sheriff's Office

b. Local Fire Department(s)

c. If you are burning during a County Burn Ban:

- County Judge

- County Commissioner's Court

- TAMFS Regional Fire Coordinator

- Texas Commission on Environmental Quality (TCEQ)

6. Is written authorization to burn during a Burn Ban attached to the burn plan OR has the County Judge signed the burn plan?

7. Have all necessary fire suppression units been briefed on the burn plan?

8. Has the Burn Boss determined risks are acceptable?

9. Has the Burn Boss obtained a current weather forecast?

10. Is the current weather within the burn plan parameters?

11. Is the fire behavior projected to be within the burn parameters?

12. Are projected fire effects acceptable?

13. Does smoke management adhere to TCEQ guidelines?
